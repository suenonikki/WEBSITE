About Us

🌟 “Together, we build awareness, compassion, and hope.” 🌟

Our Mission

We aim to break the stigma surrounding mental health in the Philippines and beyond. Through education, storytelling, and community support, we empower individuals to seek help and embrace healing.

Our Vision

A society where mental health is valued as much as physical health, where every Filipino has access to care, and where compassion guides our communities.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We listen and care deeply.
  • Empowerment: We encourage self‑care and resilience.
  • Community: We believe healing happens together.
  • Education: We spread knowledge to fight stigma.

Philippine Context

Mental health awareness is growing in the Philippines, especially after the passing of the Mental Health Act (RA 11036). We collaborate with local organizations like PMHA, UGAT Foundation, and MentalHealthPH to bring resources closer to communities.

Global Perspective

We connect with international movements like WHO’s Mental Health campaigns and World Mental Health Day to remind everyone that mental health is a universal journey.

What We Do

  • Run awareness campaigns online and offline
  • Provide educational materials and guides
  • Host community events and support groups
  • Offer interactive tools like quizzes and chat support

Get Involved

You can be part of the movement. Share your story, volunteer in community programs, or simply spread awareness by talking about mental health with friends and family.
